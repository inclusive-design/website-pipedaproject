Matthew Englander
Tuesday, 08 March 2005
Mathew Englander is a nonpractising lawyer with a special interest in information and privacy law, based in Vancouver. He acted for himself in Englander v. Telus Communications Inc., the first application under PIPEDA to be filed in Federal Court.

For more information, go to: http://www.mathew-englander.ca/
